ION Designs is a company started and created by David Tucciarone

David Tucciarone is a student of Design at Carleton Univeristy, currently outside of his education, he is working on game development, UX and UI Design, Motion graphics, Web development and much more.

David believes in Iterate, Iterate, Iterate, which is the least to say he is a problem solver and uses iteration for his designs.
